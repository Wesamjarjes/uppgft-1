Free guy

Free Guy" is a 2021 science fiction action-comedy film directed by Shawn Levy. It is a movie about an NPC named Guy who gains self-awareness in an open-world video game. He strives to become a hero, win the heart of a player character, and defy the game's impending shutdown by its developers. The film combines action, comedy, and romance, exploring themes of free will and technology's influence on our lives."
